List the file-->hdfs dfs -ls /path/to/directorymake the directory-->hdfs dfs -mkdir /path/to/new/directoryCopy the file-->hdfs dfs -copyFromLocal /path/to/local/file /path/to/hdfs/destinationhdfs dfs -copyUpload a file-->hdfs dfs -put /path/to/local/file /path/in/hdfs                hdfs dfs -copyFromLocal /path/to/local/file /path/in/hdfsDownload a file-->hdfs dfs -get /path/in/hdfs /path/to/local/fileremove the file/directory-->hdfs dfs -rm /path/in/hdfs/file_or_directory
